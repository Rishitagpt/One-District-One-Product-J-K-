Welcome to the website of
"One District One Product", J&K

The "One District One Product" (ODOP) initiative in Jammu and Kashmir is designed to stimulate local economic development by promoting unique, district-specific products across the region. This program focuses on identifying and enhancing the production and marketability of traditional and artisanal products that reflect the rich cultural heritage and natural resources of each district. By spotlighting products like Bandipora's Singhara or Anantnag's Mushqbudji Rice, ODOP aims to boost local businesses, create job opportunities, and preserve traditional crafts. This targeted approach not only supports sustainable economic growth but also fosters a sense of pride and identity within local communities by connecting them to their distinct regional products.
